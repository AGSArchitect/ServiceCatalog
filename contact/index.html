Think of the Service Catalog as your go-to guide for all the digital tools you might need for your school projects. Instead of getting overwhelmed by a long list of technical options, the catalog organizes everything into easy-to-understand categories like Storage, Databases, and Networking. You can use the simple top menu to browse around and see helpful diagrams and quick descriptions that explain exactly what each service does in plain English. It’s the perfect starting point to help you pick the right tools for your work without needing to be a tech expert first.
