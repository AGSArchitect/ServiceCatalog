Service Catalog

The mission statement of the Service Catalog is to provide actionable recommendations freely available to all university students working with cloud technologies. The website lists the most relevant services in each category, providing users with a high-level view of the options available from the cloud vendor. The catalog will include service categories such as Compute, Databases, Load Balancing, Networking, and Storage.
